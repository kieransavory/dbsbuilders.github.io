About Us

DBS Building Services - Get to know us

DBS Building Services

We are DBS Building Services. Founded in 2014, our ethos has always been about utilising local, trusted and skilled tradesmen. Operating in the Gloucestershire area. We are versatile, and are able to carry out a multitude of services, including new builds, rebuilds, renovations and refinishing. Please visit our gallery to see some examples of our capabilities.

Our in-house design specialist is an expert at visualisation of a project. With DBS Design Services, we try our best to ensure that you are catered for every step of the way, from initial conceptions to full scale ray-traced renders and wireframes, we are able to assist your needs throughout the entire process of your project.
